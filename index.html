Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application Guandan (掼蛋) for Beginners ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use it on your device.

1. No Data Collection

The App does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data. We do not gather your name, email address, location, device identifiers, usage statistics, or any other information that could identify you.

2. No Internet Connection Required

The App works entirely offline. It does not make any network requests, connect to external servers, or communicate with third-party services.

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App saves the following data only on your device, in the app's sandboxed storage area managed by iOS:

  • Game settings (language, background, character portrait, game mode, music preferences)
  • High scores (number of rounds, game mode, date of win)

This data never leaves your device and is only used to provide the game experience. It is automatically deleted when you uninstall the App.

4. No Third-Party SDKs or Analytics

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with any third party.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. It is safe for all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this Privacy Policy, the updated version will be published at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
